What Is Road Runner Email?

Road Runner Email was an email service provided by Time Warner Cable (TWC), which has since merged into Spectrum. While you can no longer create new Road Runner accounts, legacy users can still access their old accounts via Spectrum’s webmail platform.

Step-by-Step Guide to Login Your Road Runner Account

1. Go to the Official Spectrum Webmail Portal

Since Road Runner is now part of Spectrum, you need to visit:
https://webmail.spectrum.net

2. Enter Your Road Runner Email ID

  • username@rr.com
  • username@roadrunner.com
  • username@twc.com

3. Enter Your Password

Use the password you previously set up with your Road Runner account. If you've forgotten it, click "Forgot Email Password?" and follow the recovery steps.

4. Click Sign In

Once your credentials are filled, click Sign In to access your inbox, contacts, and old emails.

How to Find Your Old Road Runner Account

Here’s how to retrieve an old account:

  • Password Recovery: Use the Spectrum “Forgot Email Password” tool.
  • Contact Support: 1-833-267-6094 or visit a Spectrum store.
  • Billing Info: Provide account number or billing address to verify ownership.

Common Login Problems & Fixes

  • Incorrect credentials

  • Account deactivated due to inactivity

Accessing Road Runner Email via Mobile Devices

Incoming Mail Server (IMAP):
Server: mail.twc.com | Port: 993 | Security: SSL/TLS

Outgoing Mail Server (SMTP):
Server: mail.twc.com | Port: 587 | Security: STARTTLS

Updating Password or Security Settings

  • Go to Account Settings
  • Choose “Change Password”
  • Add recovery options

Backup Your Road Runner Emails
